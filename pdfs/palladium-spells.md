# Introduction

This document contains all spells and psionic powers for the ten spellcasting classes of Palladium Fantasy, adapted for the Shadowdark ruleset.

**How spells work in Shadowdark:** Spellcasters choose a number of spells at character creation (listed on their class card). To cast a spell, roll 1d20 plus your spellcasting modifier. On an 11+, the spell succeeds. On a 1–10, the spell fails — you still expend the attempt but gain a *Wildcard* die to add to any future roll. Spells have no slots; you may attempt any known spell each round.

**Spell tiers** reflect raw power. Tier 1 spells are learnable at character creation. Higher tiers become available as you gain levels and talents. Damage scales with tier:

| Tier | Single Target | Area Effect |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| 1    | 1d4 – 1d6    | —           |
| 2    | 2d6          | 1d6         |
| 3    | 4d6          | 3d6         |
| 4    | 6d6          | 4d6         |
| 5    | 8d6          | 8d6         |

**Saving throws** use the listed ability (CON, WIS, or STR) against DC 12 at tier 1–2, DC 13 at tier 3, DC 14 at tier 4, and DC 15 at tier 5.

# Arcane Magic

## Wizard

*Spellcasting stat: Intelligence*

Palladium Wizards are masters of invocation and elemental fire, drawing on raw arcane power to devastate enemies and unravel magical effects. They know the most spells of any class and grow only more fearsome with each tier.

### Tier 1

**Fire Bolt** · *Instant | Near*
: A bolt of fire strikes one target within near range for 1d6 damage.

**Blinding Flash** · *Instant | Near*
: A burst of white light erupts. One target makes a CON save (DC 12) or is blinded for 1d4 rounds.

**Globe of Daylight** · *1 hour | Self*
: A floating orb of bright light illuminates a near-sized area. You can move it up to near each round as a free action.

**Sense Magic** · *Focus | Near*
: You detect the presence and location of all active magic within near range.

**Repel Animals** · *Focus | Near*
: Natural animals of 2 HD or fewer will not willingly approach you for the duration.

**Shadow Cloak** · *Focus | Self*
: You blend into shadows and dim light. Advantage on stealth checks while in low-light conditions.

**Chill Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: Ice-cold energy deals 1d4 damage to one creature you touch. That creature cannot regain HP until the start of your next turn.

**Force Shield** · *Instant | Self*
: A barrier of unseen force grants you +3 AC until the start of your next turn.

**Ventriloquism** · *10 rounds | Near*
: You project your voice to appear to come from any point within near range.

**Befuddle** · *Focus | Near*
: One creature of LV 2 or less becomes too confused to act. It can only move on its turn for the duration.

### Tier 2

**Fireball (Lesser)** · *Instant | Far*
: Fire erupts in a close-sized area within far range. All creatures inside take 2d6 damage.

**Levitate** · *Focus | Self*
: You float vertically a near distance per round and can hold yourself in place by grabbing something solid.

**Invisibility** · *10 rounds | Close*
: A creature you touch becomes invisible. Ends if they attack or cast a spell.

**Wink-Out** · *Instant | Self*
: You teleport to any unoccupied space within near range you can see.

**Fear** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 12) or flees from you for 1d4 rounds.

**Carpet of Adhesion** · *5 rounds | Far*
: A near-sized floor area becomes supernaturally sticky. Creatures inside make a STR save (DC 12) each round or cannot move.

### Tier 3

**Fireball** · *Instant | Far*
: A bead of fire erupts in a near-sized area within far range. All creatures inside take 4d6 damage.

**Fly** · *5 rounds | Self*
: You fly a near distance per round.

**Dispel Magic** · *Instant | Near*
: You end one active spell on a target you can see within near range.

**Paralysis** · *Focus | Near*
: One humanoid of LV 4 or less is paralyzed. They are aware but cannot act while focused.

**Lightning Arc** · *Instant | Far*
: A bolt of lightning shoots from your hands in a straight line to far range. All creatures in the line take 3d6 damage.

### Tier 4

**Incinerate** · *Instant | Far*
: A column of fire engulfs one creature within far range for 6d6 damage. If reduced to 0 HP, the body is reduced to ash.

**Wall of Fire** · *Focus | Far*
: A near-long wall of roaring flame appears within far range. Creatures that pass through take 4d6 damage.

**Protection Circle** · *Focus | Near*
: You inscribe a magic circle. Choose one creature type (undead, demons, or natural beasts) — they cannot enter or target those inside.

**Globe of Silence** · *10 rounds | Far*
: No sound may enter or exit a near-sized sphere centered within far range.

### Tier 5

**Meteor Strike** · *Instant | Far*
: A fiery boulder crashes from above. All creatures in a near-sized area within far range take 8d6 damage.

**Annihilate** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a CON save (DC 15) or is instantly slain. On a success, they take 6d6 damage instead.

**Time Freeze** · *1d4 rounds | Self*
: Time stops for everything except you for 1d4 rounds. You may act normally; all else is frozen.

## Warlock

*Spellcasting stat: Intelligence*

Warlocks bind themselves to a single element — Air, Earth, Fire, or Water — at creation. All their power flows from that bond. Choose your element and embrace it completely.

### Tier 1

**Elemental Bolt** · *Instant | Near*
: A focused bolt of your chosen element strikes one target within near range for 1d6 damage.

**Elemental Shroud** · *Focus | Self*
: Your body radiates elemental energy. Creatures that strike you in melee take 1d4 damage of your element's type.

**Elemental Sense** · *Focus | Self*
: You sense disturbances in your element within far range and know their direction and rough distance.

**Calm Element** · *Instant | Near*
: You suppress a natural manifestation of your element within near range — still water, extinguish fire, stop wind, or halt a tremor.

**Elemental Armor** · *Focus | Self*
: Your skin crackles with elemental energy. You gain +2 AC for the duration.

**Elemental Blast** · *Instant | Near*
: A surge of elemental force hits one creature. It makes a STR save (DC 12) or is pushed near and knocked prone.

**Elemental Communication** · *Focus | Near*
: You speak to elementals of your type within near range. They understand you, though they are not compelled to cooperate.

**Call Element** · *Instant | Near*
: A near-sized volume of your element appears in an open space within near range.

**Elemental Ward** · *Focus | Self*
: You grant yourself resistance to your element for the duration, taking half damage from sources of that type.

**Minor Elemental Form** · *1 hour | Self*
: You gain one benefit based on your element — Air: fall any distance safely; Earth: ignore difficult terrain; Fire: your touch ignites flammable objects; Water: breathe and move freely underwater.

### Tier 2

**Elemental Burst** · *Instant | Near*
: Elemental energy erupts in a close-sized area within near range. All creatures inside take 2d6 damage.

**Elemental Grasp** · *Focus | Near*
: Tendrils of your element seize one creature within near range. It makes a STR save (DC 12) or is restrained for the duration.

**Summon Lesser Elemental** · *10 rounds | Close*
: A lesser elemental of your type appears and obeys you. It has HP equal to twice your level and deals 1d6 damage per strike.

**Elemental Speed** · *5 rounds | Self*
: You move with supernatural swiftness. Your speed doubles and you may move and act in the same round without penalty.

**Elemental Shape** · *5 rounds | Self*
: You gain a physical quality of your element — Air: fly near/round; Earth: +3 AC and ignore knockback; Fire: melee attacks deal +1d6 fire damage; Water: move through water freely and breathe underwater.

### Tier 3

**Elemental Torrent** · *Instant | Far*
: A massive surge of your element blasts a near-sized area within far range. All creatures inside take 3d6 damage.

**Summon Elemental** · *1 hour | Close*
: A full elemental of your type appears within close range. It has HP equal to 5 times your level, deals 2d6 damage per strike, and serves for 1 hour.

**Elemental Body** · *10 rounds | Self*
: You become partially elemental. You are immune to your chosen element, and your melee attacks deal +1d6 elemental damage.

**Elemental Control** · *Focus | Far*
: You command a near-sized body of your element to move and shape itself as you direct — reshape a river, form a wall of stone, direct a fire's path, etc.

### Tier 4

**Elemental Storm** · *5 rounds | Far*
: A howling storm of your element fills a near-sized area within far range. All creatures inside take 4d6 damage per round.

**Summon Greater Elemental** · *Focus | Close*
: A towering elemental of your type appears. It has HP equal to 8 times your level, deals 3d6 damage per strike, and serves until dismissed.

**Elemental Avatar** · *10 rounds | Self*
: You transform into a being of pure elemental force. AC 16, immune to your element, melee attacks deal an additional 2d6 elemental damage.

### Tier 5

**Cataclysm** · *Instant | Far*
: Your element erupts catastrophically. All creatures in a near-sized area take 8d6 damage and suffer an element-specific condition — Air: deafened 1 day; Earth: restrained under rubble; Fire: burning for 2d6/round until extinguished; Water: drowning (incapacitated).

**Elemental Ascension** · *1 hour | Self*
: You become a true elemental. AC 18, immune to all non-magical damage and your element, fly or burrow near each round, melee attacks deal 4d6 elemental damage.

## Witch

*Spellcasting stat: Intelligence*

Witches command dark nature magic: hexes, curses, poisons, and the power of life and death. They are feared across the world for good reason. Their magic is subtle or devastating depending on their mood.

### Tier 1

**Mend Flesh** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 1d4 HP.

**Cause Fear** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 12) or flees from you for 1d4 rounds.

**Befriend Animal** · *1 hour | Near*
: One natural animal of 2 HD or fewer becomes friendly toward you for 1 hour and will follow simple requests.

**Spoil** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch food or drink. It becomes foul — anyone who consumes it has disadvantage on all rolls for 1 hour (CON save DC 12 to resist).

**Commune with Spirits** · *Instant | Self*
: You ask one question of a nearby spirit or ancestral presence. The GM answers as best the spirit knows.

**Cackle Curse** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature within near range makes a WIS save (DC 12) or falls into helpless laughter for 1 round, unable to act.

**Nightvision** · *1 hour | Self*
: You see clearly in total darkness to near range for the duration.

**Poison Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: A creature you touch makes a CON save (DC 12) or takes 1d6 poison damage per round for 1d4 rounds.

**Witch Sight** · *Focus | Near*
: You see invisible creatures, detect active magic, and sense curses on creatures or objects within near range.

**Entangle** · *5 rounds | Near*
: Vines and roots erupt in a close-sized area within near range. All creatures inside make a STR save (DC 12) or are restrained.

**Sleep** · *Instant | Near*
: You weave a lulling hex. Up to 2 creatures of LV 2 or less that you can see within near range fall into deep sleep. Injury wakes them.

**Object Read** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch an object and receive impressions of its recent history — who held it, strong emotions, and major events in its presence.

### Tier 2

**Evil Eye** · *Focus | Near*
: You fix your gaze on one creature within near range. It has disadvantage on all rolls for the duration. CON save (DC 12) to resist.

**Polymorph (Hex)** · *5 rounds | Near*
: You transform one creature of LV 3 or less into a small animal of your choice. CON save (DC 12) resists. Reverts when it takes damage.

**Mind Bond** · *Focus | Close*
: You link your senses to a willing creature you touch. You see and hear through them for the duration.

**Shadow Walk** · *Instant | Self*
: You and up to two willing creatures step into shadows and emerge from any shadow you can see within far range.

**Poison Mist** · *5 rounds | Near*
: A cloud of greenish vapour fills a close-sized area within near range. All creatures inside take 2d6 poison damage per round.

### Tier 3

**Slay Living** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature within near range makes a CON save (DC 13) or takes 4d6 necrotic damage. If slain, it cannot be raised as undead.

**Summon Demons** · *1 hour | Close*
: You call 1d4 lesser demons that serve you for 1 hour. Each has 8 HP and deals 1d6 damage per strike.

**Flesh to Stone** · *Focus | Near*
: One creature within near range makes a CON save (DC 13) or turns to stone. Ends when you dispel it or they succeed on a nightly save.

**Speak with Dead** · *10 minutes | Close*
: A corpse within close range answers your questions for 10 minutes. It knows only what it knew in life and must answer truthfully.

### Tier 4

**Death Curse** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature you can see makes a CON save (DC 14) or is reduced to 0 HP at the start of its next turn. On a success, it takes 6d6 damage instead.

**Animate Dead** · *1 day | Near*
: You animate 1d4 corpses within near range as zombies or skeletons that obey your commands.

**Gate** · *10 rounds | Near*
: You tear open a rift summoning a powerful demon (LV equal to yours +2). It serves for 10 rounds before returning.

### Tier 5

**Pestilence** · *1 week | Close*
: A supernatural plague spreads from one creature you touch (CON save DC 15 or infected). The disease spreads to anyone the creature touches unless they succeed the same save.

**Apocalyptic Curse** · *Focus | Near*
: One creature has disadvantage on all rolls, takes 2d6 damage at the start of each of its turns, and cannot regain HP. Lasts until dispelled.

# Divine Magic

## Priest of Light

*Spellcasting stat: Wisdom*

Priests of Light serve the gods of goodness and law. They heal the wounded, banish the undead, and call down holy wrath on the servants of darkness. They are the shield of civilization.

### Tier 1

**Healing Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 1d6 HP.

**Holy Light** · *Instant | Near*
: A burst of radiant light fills a near-sized area. Undead and demons within take 1d6 radiant damage and have disadvantage on all rolls until the start of your next turn.

**Bless** · *10 rounds | Near*
: Up to three creatures you can see gain +1 to attack rolls and saving throws for the duration.

**Protection from Evil** · *Focus | Close*
: Chaotic and demonic beings have disadvantage on attacks and hostile spellcasting checks against one creature you touch.

**Detect Evil** · *Focus | Self*
: You sense the presence and rough direction of evil, undead, or demonic creatures within far range.

**Light** · *1 hour | Close*
: An object you touch glows with bright light that fills a near area. Touch it again to end the spell.

**Sanctuary** · *1 round | Close*
: One creature you touch cannot be targeted by attacks for 1 round unless it attacks first.

**Purify** · *Instant | Close*
: You purify all food, water, and air within close range, removing poison, disease, and curses.

### Tier 2

**Cure Disease** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and remove one disease, poison, or curse afflicting it.

**Hold Undead** · *Focus | Near*
: Undead within near range make a WIS save (DC 12) or are paralyzed for the duration.

**Restoration** · *Instant | Close*
: You remove one ability score penalty, condition (blinded, deafened, poisoned), or curse from a creature you touch.

**Shield of Faith** · *Focus | Close*
: A shimmering divine field surrounds one creature you touch, granting +2 AC for the duration.

**Consecrate** · *1 day | Near*
: You sanctify a near-sized area. Undead and demons entering it take 1d6 radiant damage each round they remain inside.

### Tier 3

**Mass Healing** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to four creatures within near range each recover 2d6 HP.

**Exorcism** · *Instant | Close*
: You expel a possessing entity, ghost, or demon from a creature or place. It makes a WIS save (DC 13) or is cast out and barred for 1 day.

**Sunburst** · *Instant | Near*
: A beam of brilliant holy light strikes all undead and demons within a near-sized area, each taking 4d6 radiant damage. Living creatures are unharmed.

**Commune** · *Instant | Self*
: You ask your deity three yes or no questions. The GM answers truthfully.

### Tier 4

**Resurrection** · *Instant | Close*
: You restore a dead creature to life. It returns with 1 HP. Cannot restore those dead more than 1 week or whose bodies are destroyed.

**Holy Aura** · *10 rounds | Near*
: All creatures you choose within near range gain +4 AC, advantage on saves, and immunity to charm and fear for the duration.

**Banish Demon** · *Instant | Near*
: One demon or devil within near range makes a WIS save (DC 14) or is instantly returned to its plane of origin and barred for 1 year.

### Tier 5

**Miracle** · *Instant | Self*
: You petition your deity. One impossible thing happens — a creature restored to full HP, a curse lifted from a region, or a question answered. GM's discretion.

**Divine Wrath** · *Instant | Far*
: Blazing holy fire rains on a near-sized area within far range. All creatures take 8d6 radiant damage. Undead and demons take double.

## Priest of Darkness

*Spellcasting stat: Wisdom*

Priests of Darkness serve the gods of evil and entropy. They command the dead, spread fear, and wield necrotic power. Not all are mindlessly cruel — but all are dangerous.

### Tier 1

**Dark Healing Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature (including undead) and restore 1d6 HP.

**Cause Fear** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature within near range makes a WIS save (DC 12) or flees from you for 1d4 rounds.

**Darkness** · *10 rounds | Near*
: You snuff all light in a near-sized area for the duration. Creatures within cannot see anything.

**Deathly Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: A chill passes into a creature you touch. It makes a CON save (DC 12) or takes 1d6 necrotic damage and cannot regain HP until the start of your next turn.

**Sense Evil** · *Focus | Self*
: You detect the presence and location of evil, undead, and demonic creatures within far range.

**Curse** · *1 hour | Near*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 12) or takes -2 to all rolls for 1 hour.

**Animate Corpse** · *1 day | Close*
: You animate one nearby corpse as a skeleton or zombie that follows simple commands.

**Unholy Presence** · *Focus | Near*
: Creatures of 1 HD or fewer that can see you must make a WIS save (DC 12) or be frightened and unable to approach you.

### Tier 2

**Wither** · *1 day | Near*
: One creature makes a CON save (DC 12) or one ability score of your choice is reduced by 2 for 1 day.

**Speak with Dead** · *Instant | Close*
: You call the spirit of a nearby corpse. It answers up to three questions truthfully before departing.

**Darkness Wave** · *Instant | Near*
: A wave of supernatural darkness rolls outward from you. All non-undead, non-demon creatures within near range take 2d6 damage and are blinded for 1 round.

**Bone Cage** · *5 rounds | Near*
: Bones erupt from nearby remains and cage one creature within near range. It makes a STR save (DC 12) or is restrained for the duration.

**Death's Embrace** · *1 hour | Close*
: You touch a willing creature. It appears and registers as dead to all magical detection and mundane examination for 1 hour.

### Tier 3

**Slay Living** · *Instant | Near*
: One living creature makes a CON save (DC 13) or takes 4d6 necrotic damage. If slain, it rises immediately as a zombie under your control.

**Mass Fear** · *Instant | Near*
: All living creatures within a near-sized area make a WIS save (DC 12) or flee in terror for 1d4 rounds.

**Control Undead** · *1 hour | Near*
: You seize control of up to 1d6 undead within near range. They obey your commands for 1 hour, then revert.

**Pestilence** · *Focus | Close*
: One creature you touch makes a CON save (DC 13) or contracts a supernatural wasting disease. It takes 1d6 necrotic damage per hour and cannot recover HP until cured.

### Tier 4

**Death Spell** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to 2d4 HD of living creatures within near range must make CON saves (DC 14) or die, targeting weakest first.

**Bone Golem** · *1 day | Close*
: You animate a mass of bones into a powerful construct. It has HP equal to 4 times your level, deals 2d6 damage per strike, and obeys your commands.

**Lich Touch** · *Focus | Close*
: One creature you touch makes a CON save (DC 14) or begins to wither, losing 1d6 HP per round and unable to recover HP while focused.

### Tier 5

**Apocalypse** · *Instant | Far*
: A wave of necrotic death washes over a near-sized area within far range. All living creatures take 8d6 necrotic damage. Those slain rise immediately as zombies under your control.

**True Death** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a CON save (DC 15) or is slain outright. Its body crumbles to dust, preventing any form of resurrection.

## Druid

*Spellcasting stat: Wisdom*

Druids are priests of the natural world, standing apart from both the Church of Light and Darkness. They answer to no god but nature itself — healing, shaping the land, and commanding storms and beasts.

### Tier 1

**Healing Herbs** · *Instant | Close*
: You call upon nature to heal. One creature you touch recovers 1d6 HP.

**Entangle** · *5 rounds | Near*
: Vines and roots erupt in a close-sized area within near range. All creatures inside make a STR save (DC 12) or are restrained.

**Speak with Animals** · *Focus | Near*
: You communicate freely with natural animals within near range for the duration.

**Thunderclap** · *Instant | Close*
: A crack of thunder shakes the air. All creatures within close range (except yourself) take 1d4 damage and are deafened for 1 round.

**Barkskin** · *Focus | Close*
: One creature you touch gains +2 AC as their skin hardens like bark.

**Pass Without Trace** · *Focus | Self*
: You and up to four willing creatures leave no tracks, scent, or sound. Advantage on stealth checks for the duration.

**Faerie Fire** · *Focus | Near*
: Objects and creatures in a near-sized area glow with dim light. Affected creatures cannot benefit from invisibility, and attack rolls against them have advantage.

**Animal Form** · *1 hour | Self*
: You transform into any natural animal up to wolf-sized. You retain your WIS but otherwise use the animal's stats.

### Tier 2

**Summon Animal** · *10 rounds | Close*
: A loyal natural animal (wolf, bear, eagle, or boar) of your choice appears within close range and fights alongside you.

**Call Lightning** · *Focus | Far*
: Clouds gather and lightning strikes one creature within far range for 2d6 damage. You can call one bolt per round while focusing.

**Cure Disease** · *Instant | Close*
: You remove one disease, poison, or curse from a creature you touch.

**Warp Wood** · *Instant | Near*
: Wood within near range twists and warps. Wooden weapons deal -1 damage, wooden doors burst or jam, wooden structures crack.

**Stone Shape** · *Instant | Close*
: You reshape a near-sized volume of stone however you wish — open a passage, seal a door, or raise a low wall.

### Tier 3

**Call of the Wild** · *1 hour | Near*
: 1d6 natural animals of appropriate size appear and fight for you for 1 hour.

**Earthquake** · *Instant | Far*
: The ground shakes in a far-sized area. All creatures on the ground make a DEX save (DC 13) or fall prone. Structures crack; tunnels may collapse.

**Wind Wall** · *Focus | Far*
: A wall of howling wind stretches near in length. Ranged attacks through it automatically miss. Flying creatures must make STR saves (DC 12) or be blown away.

**Control Weather** · *1 hour | Self*
: You alter the weather in a mile-wide area for 1 hour — call rain, clear skies, fog, strong wind, or light snow.

### Tier 4

**Mass Cure** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to four creatures within near range each recover 3d6 HP.

**Creeping Doom** · *5 rounds | Far*
: A writhing mass of insects fills a near-sized area within far range. All creatures inside take 4d6 damage per round.

**Greater Animal Form** · *1 hour | Self*
: You transform into any natural animal up to elephant-sized. You retain your WIS and may revert and re-transform freely within the duration.

### Tier 5

**Nature's Wrath** · *Instant | Far*
: A pillar of primal energy erupts in a near-sized area within far range. All creatures take 8d6 damage. Plants and animals in the area are unharmed and enraged against anything that survived.

**Reincarnate** · *Instant | Close*
: You restore a dead creature to life in a new body — a random humanoid ancestry determined by the GM. Returns with full HP and no memories of death.

# Psionics

## Psychic Sensitive

*Spellcasting stat: Charisma*

Psychic Sensitives perceive what others cannot: emotions, lies, spirits, and the threads of fate. They are seers and detectives, capable of devastating mental attacks at higher tiers.

### Tier 1

**Sense Minds** · *Focus | Near*
: You sense the presence and rough emotions of all thinking creatures within near range. You know if they are hostile, afraid, or calm.

**Read Aura** · *Instant | Near*
: You read one creature's aura. Learn their general power level, alignment, and whether they bear any active magical or psionic effects.

**Commune with Dead** · *Instant | Near*
: You sense and speak with a nearby spirit or ghost. It may answer up to three questions truthfully before departing.

**Resist Psychic** · *Focus | Self*
: You steel your mind. Advantage on saves against psionic attacks, charm, possession, and mind control for the duration.

**Detect Deception** · *Focus | Close*
: You know when a creature within close range intentionally lies for the duration.

**Psychic Alarm** · *1 day | Close*
: You attune to a room or object within close range. You instantly know when a creature enters the room or touches the object.

**Sixth Sense** · *10 rounds | Self*
: You sense danger moments before it arrives. You cannot be surprised, and you have advantage on the first attack roll or save each round.

**Object Read** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch an object and receive impressions of its recent history — who held it, strong emotions, and major events in its presence.

**Sense Supernatural** · *Focus | Self*
: You detect supernatural entities (ghosts, demons, undead, and beings of magic) within far range and know their direction and distance.

**Mind Shield** · *1 hour | Self*
: Other creatures cannot read your thoughts, detect your presence psionically, or sense your aura while this is active.

### Tier 2

**Telepathy** · *Focus | Near*
: You read the surface thoughts of one creature within near range. Each round you focus, the GM tells you its immediate thoughts.

**Clairvoyance** · *Focus | Self*
: You see and hear through a point you can visualize within 1 mile, as if standing there invisibly.

**Mind Link** · *1 hour | Close*
: You form a silent telepathic bond with up to three willing creatures. You may communicate wordlessly with them at any range.

**Psychic Blast** · *Instant | Near*
: A wave of psychic force strikes one creature for 2d6 psychic damage. It makes a WIS save (DC 12) or is stunned until the start of your next turn.

**Astral Projection** · *Focus | Self*
: Your spirit leaves your body and travels invisibly up to 1 mile. You see and hear normally but cannot interact physically. Your body is defenseless while projected.

### Tier 3

**Domination** · *1 hour | Near*
: One humanoid of LV 4 or less makes a WIS save (DC 13) or is under your mental command for 1 hour. It won't harm itself.

**Group Mind** · *1 hour | Near*
: You link up to eight willing creatures in a shared mental network. All share sensory information and can communicate instantly.

**Psionic Scream** · *Instant | Near*
: A wave of psychic agony radiates from you. All creatures within near range take 3d6 psychic damage and are stunned for 1 round (WIS save DC 12 halves damage and negates stun).

**Precognition** · *10 rounds | Self*
: You experience flashes of the immediate future. You cannot be surprised and have advantage on the first attack roll or save each round.

### Tier 4

**Mass Domination** · *10 rounds | Near*
: Up to 1d4 humanoids of LV 4 or less within near range each make a WIS save (DC 14) or come under your mental control.

**Mind Crush** · *Instant | Near*
: You assault one creature's mind with devastating force. It takes 6d6 psychic damage and makes a WIS save (DC 14) or is incapacitated for 1d4 rounds.

**Psychic Possession** · *1 hour | Close*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 14) or you control its body for 1 hour. Your own body collapses unconscious while possessing.

### Tier 5

**Total Mind Wipe** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 15) or all memories of a subject of your choice are permanently erased.

**Mass Psionic Assault** · *Instant | Near*
: All creatures within a near-sized area take 8d6 psychic damage and make a WIS save (DC 15) or are incapacitated for 1d4 rounds.

## Psi-Healer

*Spellcasting stat: Charisma*

Psi-Healers focus their psychic talent entirely on the body — mending wounds, curing disease, relieving pain, and eventually reversing death itself. They are the rarest and most valued psionics.

### Tier 1

**Healing Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 1d6 HP through focused psionic energy.

**Deaden Pain** · *1 hour | Close*
: One creature you touch cannot feel pain for 1 hour. They are immune to pain penalties and can act normally even at 0 HP until the spell ends (then collapse).

**Psychic Diagnosis** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and instantly know their current HP, any diseases, poisons, or curses affecting them, and whether they are near death.

**Empathy** · *Instant | Near*
: You send calming psychic energy to one creature within near range. It immediately loses the frightened, charmed, or berserk condition.

**Stabilize** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a dying creature at 0 HP. It immediately stabilizes and no longer loses HP.

**Cleanse Poison** · *Instant | Close*
: You remove one poison or disease from a creature you touch.

**Boost** · *10 rounds | Close*
: One creature you touch gains advantage on STR, DEX, or CON checks (your choice) for the duration.

**Psychic Bandage** · *10 rounds | Close*
: Wounds on a creature you touch close visibly. They recover 1d4 HP now and 1d4 HP at the start of your next turn.

**Pain Strike** · *Instant | Near*
: You project agony into one creature within near range. It takes 1d6 psychic damage and has disadvantage on its next attack roll (CON save DC 12 negates disadvantage).

**Share Sense** · *10 rounds | Close*
: You link your senses to a willing creature you touch. You see and hear through them (and they through you) for the duration.

### Tier 2

**Greater Healing** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 2d6 HP.

**Cure Paralysis** · *Instant | Close*
: You restore full movement to one paralyzed, restrained, or slowed creature you touch.

**Mass Empathy** · *Instant | Near*
: All creatures within near range that you choose immediately lose one negative condition (frightened, charmed, or enraged).

**Psychic Surgery** · *Instant | Close*
: You perform rapid mental healing. Remove one of: broken bone, internal injury, blindness, deafness, or a non-magical disease from a creature you touch.

**Trauma Shield** · *5 rounds | Close*
: One creature you touch cannot drop below 1 HP for the duration. Damage that would reduce them below 1 HP is negated instead.

### Tier 3

**Mass Healing** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to four creatures within near range each recover 2d6 HP.

**Restoration** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and remove all conditions, curses, poisons, and diseases affecting it.

**Regenerate** · *10 rounds | Close*
: One creature you touch regains HP equal to your level at the start of each of their turns for the duration.

**Resurrect** · *Instant | Close*
: You restore a recently dead creature (dead less than 1 hour) to life. They return at 1 HP on a successful CHA check (DC 13).

### Tier 4

**Full Restoration** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and fully restore their HP, remove all conditions, and end all curses and diseases.

**Mass Restoration** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to four creatures within near range recover 3d6 HP each and are freed of all conditions, poisons, and diseases.

**Sever Pain** · *Instant | Near*
: You project psionic agony into one creature. It takes 4d6 psychic damage and is incapacitated with pain for 1d4 rounds (CON save DC 14 negates incapacitation).

### Tier 5

**True Resurrection** · *Instant | Close*
: You restore a dead creature to life with full HP, regardless of how long they have been dead or the condition of their remains.

**Mass Full Restoration** · *Instant | Near*
: All allies within near range are restored to full HP and freed of all conditions, curses, and diseases.

## Psi-Mystic

*Spellcasting stat: Charisma*

Psi-Mystics blend psionic ability with a spiritual awareness of the unseen world. They sense spirits, navigate the astral plane, and perceive truths that are hidden from ordinary sight.

### Tier 1

**Sense Evil** · *Focus | Self*
: You detect the presence and rough location of evil-aligned beings, undead, and demons within far range.

**Sixth Sense** · *10 rounds | Self*
: You sense danger moments before it arrives. You cannot be surprised and have advantage on initiative rolls for the duration.

**See Aura** · *Instant | Near*
: You read one creature or object's aura: power level, alignment, active magic or psionic effects, and emotional state.

**Mind Block** · *Focus | Self*
: Creatures cannot read your thoughts, detect your presence psionically, or sense your aura while this is active.

**Commune with Spirit** · *Instant | Near*
: You speak with a ghost or spirit within near range. It must answer three questions to the best of its knowledge, then departs.

**Meditation** · *10 minutes | Self*
: You enter focused meditation for 10 minutes. Recover one expended spell slot and gain advantage on your next WIS or CHA check.

**Healing Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 1d4 HP through psionic focus.

**Resist Magic** · *Focus | Close*
: You grant yourself or one creature you touch resistance to one school of magic (choose when casting). Treat affected spells as dealing half damage.

**Astral Sense** · *Focus | Self*
: You extend your senses into the astral plane, seeing astral entities, auras, and magical constructs overlaid on the physical world.

**Detect Psionic** · *Instant | Near*
: You sense whether any creature within near range has psionic ability, and roughly how powerful.

**Empathic Reading** · *1 minute | Close*
: By spending one minute near a creature, you learn its general emotional state, whether it is lying, and one dominant thought or concern.

**Object Sense** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch an object and receive impressions of its recent history — significant events, strong emotions, who last held it.

### Tier 2

**Telepathy** · *Focus | Near*
: You read the surface thoughts of one creature within near range. Each round you focus, the GM tells you its immediate thoughts.

**Exorcism** · *Instant | Close*
: You expel a possessing entity or spirit from a creature or place. It makes a WIS save (DC 12) or is cast out.

**Psychic Blast** · *Instant | Near*
: A psionic shockwave strikes one creature for 2d6 psychic damage. It makes a WIS save (DC 12) or is stunned for 1 round.

**Clairvoyance** · *Focus | Self*
: You see and hear through any point you can visualize within 1 mile, as if standing there invisibly.

**Mind Link** · *1 hour | Close*
: You form a silent mental bond with up to three willing creatures, communicating wordlessly at any range.

**Banish Spirit** · *Instant | Near*
: One ghost, spirit, or ethereal entity within near range makes a WIS save (DC 13) or is expelled to the afterlife or its plane of origin.

### Tier 3

**Mass Sense** · *Instant | Near*
: You read the aura and surface thoughts of all creatures within near range simultaneously.

**Plane Shift** · *Instant | Close*
: You and one willing creature you touch are transported to the astral plane or back to the physical plane.

**Greater Exorcism** · *Instant | Near*
: You can expel possessing entities from up to your level in creatures simultaneously. Each makes a WIS save (DC 13) or the entity is expelled.

**Psionic Shield** · *Focus | Close*
: A protective psionic field surrounds one creature you touch, granting +4 AC and advantage on saves against magic and psionics.

### Tier 4

**Soul Sense** · *Instant | Close*
: You read the deepest nature of one creature within close range — its true alignment, secret fears, hidden agenda, and most guarded memory. No save.

**Astral Fortress** · *Focus | Near*
: You create a psionic barrier in a near-sized area. No psionic or magical effect can penetrate it while you focus.

**Mass Banishment** · *Instant | Near*
: All spirits, ghosts, and ethereal entities within near range make WIS saves (DC 14) or are immediately expelled.

### Tier 5

**Cosmic Sight** · *1 round | Self*
: You see all things simultaneously — all creatures within 1 mile, their auras, intentions, and hidden truths. During this round you cannot be deceived, surprised, or caught unaware.

**Soul Anchor** · *Focus | Close*
: You bind a dying creature's spirit. It cannot depart until you release it or the spell ends. The creature remains at -1 HP and cannot die while anchored.

## Mind Mage

*Spellcasting stat: Charisma*

Mind Mages are the most powerful and versatile psychics. They combine telekinesis, telepathy, healing, and mental combat into a complete psionic discipline. A master Mind Mage is one of the most formidable beings alive.

### Tier 1

**Mind Bolt** · *Instant | Near*
: A focused burst of mental force strikes one creature within near range for 1d6 psychic damage.

**Telekinetic Push** · *Instant | Near*
: You hurl one object or creature psionically. Objects up to 50 lbs fly near in any direction. Creatures make a STR save (DC 12) or are pushed near and knocked prone.

**Mind Block** · *Focus | Self*
: Creatures cannot read your thoughts, sense your presence psionically, affect you with charm, or attempt possession while this is active.

**See Aura** · *Instant | Near*
: You read one creature or object's aura: power level, alignment, emotional state, and any active magical or psionic effects.

**Psionic Sense** · *Instant | Near*
: You detect whether any creature within near range has psionic ability and roughly how powerful it is.

**Meditation** · *10 minutes | Self*
: You center yourself in 10 minutes of focus. Recover one expended spell slot and clear your mind of confusion, charm, or fear.

**Telekinetic Lift** · *Focus | Near*
: You lift one object within near range weighing up to 100 lbs and hold it aloft, moving it a close distance per round while focused.

**Empathic Link** · *Instant | Close*
: You form a brief psychic connection with one creature within close range. You sense its precise emotional state and whether it is hiding something.

**Healing Touch** · *Instant | Close*
: You touch a creature and restore 1d4 HP through focused psionic energy.

**Alter Aura** · *1 hour | Self*
: You mask your true aura from psychic detection. You may appear to be any alignment, any psionic strength, or entirely mundane.

### Tier 2

**Telepathy** · *Focus | Near*
: You read the surface thoughts of one creature within near range. Each round you focus, the GM tells you its immediate thoughts.

**Telekinetic Grip** · *Focus | Near*
: You seize one creature within near range in a psionic vice. It makes a STR save (DC 12) or is restrained for the duration.

**Psychic Blast** · *Instant | Near*
: A psionic shockwave strikes one creature for 2d6 psychic damage. It makes a WIS save (DC 12) or is stunned for 1 round.

**Psi-Sword** · *Instant | Close*
: You manifest a blade of psionic force. It ignores physical armor (treat target as AC 10) and deals 1d8 + your CHA modifier damage.

**Minor Mind Control** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature of LV 2 or less makes a WIS save (DC 12) or follows a simple verbal command (one action).

### Tier 3

**Telekinetic Barrage** · *Instant | Near*
: You hurl up to four objects within near range simultaneously, each striking a different target for 1d6 damage each.

**Domination** · *1 hour | Near*
: One humanoid of LV 4 or less makes a WIS save (DC 13) or is under your mental command for 1 hour. It won't harm itself.

**Mass Healing** · *Instant | Near*
: Up to four creatures within near range each recover 2d6 HP through psionic restoration.

**Psionic Drain** · *Instant | Near*
: You drain psionic or magical energy from one creature. It loses one spell slot (or equivalent) and you recover 1d6 HP.

### Tier 4

**Possess** · *1 hour | Close*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 14) or you control its body for 1 hour. Your body collapses unconscious while possessing.

**Telekinetic Storm** · *Instant | Near*
: You hurl everything in the environment at creatures in a near-sized area. All creatures inside take 4d6 damage.

**Mass Mind Control** · *10 rounds | Near*
: Up to 1d4 humanoids of LV 4 or less within near range each make a WIS save (DC 14) or are under your mental command.

### Tier 5

**Mind Wipe** · *Instant | Near*
: One creature makes a WIS save (DC 15) or all memories of a period or subject of your choice are permanently erased.

**Total Domination** · *1 hour | Near*
: Up to six creatures you can see within near range each make a WIS save (DC 15) or fall under your mental control for 1 hour.
